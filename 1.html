Indoor Water Saving

Making adjustments to your everyday water usage may sound scary. But there is no need to feel like a fish out of water! Saving water indoors is easy and painless. And there are so many opportunities to save, whether it’s in your kitchen, bathroom, or laundry room. The smallest adjustments can save you a good deal of water… and money!
